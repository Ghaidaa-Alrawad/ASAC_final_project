Bookshelf

Looking for a book? This is the right place, go ahead and search for your favorite book, or explore a new one.

Why This Project?

The Bookshelf Library Project website is a testament to my work on the ASAC fall training with the instructor Boshra Jaber. Under Boshra's expert guidance, I was able to build this website using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and REST API to bring the book's picture, name, and year of publishing.

You know what is the best part? No gym membership required, just your device and a comfy chair.

So why not give it a try and become a bookworm today!
